The Softest Bread in South Slope.

We make Japanese Shokupan using the traditional Yudane method. It's incredibly soft, slightly sweet, and keeps fresh for days. Toast it, sandwich it, or just tear it apart and eat it plain.

Why Shokupan?

We're not a big commercial bakery. We're a small kitchen in Brooklyn obsessed with texture. Most bread dries out in a day. Ours specifically uses a hot water roux (Yudane) to lock in moisture, giving you that distinct stretch and bounce.

It's simple, honest white bread, but better. Perfect for a Saturday morning French toast or a school lunch sandwich that actually gets eaten.
